# Customizations

* Homerow mods, GACS
* "Flips": SHIFT behaviour is flipped
  * **&fl_0 - &fl_9**: Flip numbers. Numbers require SHIFT, symbols take precedence.
  * **&fl_pipe**: Flip PIPE and BACKSLASH.
  * **&fl_quote**: Flip DQUOTE and APOS.
* **&tdsc**: SHIFT SHIFT is CAPSLOCK.
* **&bsp_del**: SHIFT+BSP is DELETE.
* **DELETE** is **TAB**
* **td_kp**: holding momentarily activates KP layer, tapping twice activates layer permanently.
* **td_quote**: double-tap expands to **"\<CURSOR>"** or **'\<CURSOR>'**
