# Customizations

* Homerow mods, [GACS](https://precondition.github.io/home-row-mods#gacs)
* "Flips": SHIFT behaviour is flipped
  * **&fl_0 - &fl_9**: Flip numbers. Numbers require SHIFT, symbols take precedence.
  * **&pipe**: Flip PIPE and BACKSLASH.
  * **&quote**: Flip DQUOTE and APOS.
* Keymaps:
    * **&shit_caps**: SHIFT SHIFT is CAPSLOCK.
    * **&bsp_del**: SHIFT+BSP is DELETE.
    * **DELETE** is **TAB**.
* **kp_tog**: holding momentarily activates KP layer, tapping twice activates layer permanently.
* Tap for pairs
  * **quotes**: double-tap expands to **"\<CURSOR>"** or **'\<CURSOR>'**
